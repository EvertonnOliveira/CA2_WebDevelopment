Why This Website Exists

A problem worth solving and the thinking behind our approach.

Many international job seekers struggle to find positions in Ireland that offer visa sponsorship. Searching on platforms like LinkedIn or Indeed requires reading each job description individually there is no "Sponsorship Available" filter.

This wastes hours of effort and creates frustration for candidates who are fully qualified but need an employer willing to sponsor their work permit under Ireland's Critical Skills Employment Permit programme.

Irish Sponsorship Jobs solves this by curating only roles that explicitly include sponsorship saving candidates time and helping qualified internationals find their path to working in Ireland.
