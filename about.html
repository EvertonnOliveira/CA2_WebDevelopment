Why This Website Exists

Many job seekers struggle to find positions in Ireland that offer visa sponsorship.

Platforms like LinkedIn and Indeed do not provide a filter for "Sponsorship Available", making the search slow and frustrating for international candidates.

This website solves that problem by listing only jobs that include Critical Skills sponsorship, helping international candidates find relevant opportunities faster.
